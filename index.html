Real Advert is a targeted messaging service that operates across all types of digital devices.

This patented solution:

Is designed for any legal entity with a customer database and a media channel.

Enables the monetization of customer databases without disclosing them, ensuring their security permanently.

Data, the gold of the 21st century

Why Real Advert?

Observation:

The advertising market is based on flawed targeting mechanisms:

Data "harvested" on the edge of legality, often unverified, leading to inaccurate targeting.

Continuous tracking techniques (profiling and tracking) that harm privacy and trust, are costly (data storage, security).

Behavioral or audience extrapolations that are open to debate.

An uncertain ad reception rate and unguaranteed audience attention

Outcome :

Publishers1 lose control of their own data.

Advertisers2 obtain variable conversion rates.

Users feel surveilled and are subjected to an overload of intrusive messages.

1 Entities that operate and manage media.; 2 Entities that communicate.
